2. Practices.csv

50 practices of paddy
Operational Costs: in INR/ha/season
Note: One thing we forgot to modeling of the operating costs is its comparison with the base line costs, some times net costs may not be negative, it can be positive because of savings. eg. Mulching operating cost is there, but it can save money from base line costs. So included that term of savings from the base line costs.

Notes on the numbers I assigned (logic)

AWD/MSD/drainage rules → low outlay, moderate savings (water/pumping + fewer irrigations).

Laser leveling / bund repair / drainage outlets → higher outlay (service/rental), some savings.

DSR / dry seeding → moderate outlay (weed mgmt etc.) but high savings (reduced transplant/puddling labor).

Inhibitors / CRU → higher outlay, low savings (more “pay to reduce emissions”).

Biochar → very high outlay, small savings (mostly carbon benefit rather than cost-saving).
